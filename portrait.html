My responsibility isn't to paint a flattering portrait; my responsibility is to paint a real portrait, a true portrait.

When we at Tography discover a beautiful subject to be photographed, we try our best to get all our best moral and physical characteristics out of our model.

When we take charge of creating a reportage of a subject we love to create immediately a structured project on the emotions that our client wants to show and, therefore, we choose type of photo, location and intrinsic symbolism, agreeing all these aspects first to be in line perfect with the needs of our customer.

Our method

To get the most from our subject it is imperative for us to know it thoroughly before starting to work in order to be able to make it feel at ease behind our camera body; it is a very important phase in order to make sure that the model gives its best, giving us expressions and poses the most natural and sincere possible, guaranteeing an exceptional result!

We can not be content with trivial photos and oriented to mediocrity, as most photographers of these times do, certainly not for their fault, but because of a society oriented to productivity and not to quality, but the true works of art are those that require time and dedication to be developed and not speed and technology.

How can I help you

We are very attracted to the face and facial expressions, so we want a lot of gestures and expressiveness in our photographs, which, without establishing an initial relationship of mutual trust, can never be expressed by a photograph taken 'cold'.

After a careful analysis of the client's requests, an initial project and a series of reports, we will bring the material to our studio where we will elaborate the best with the professional tools of photo editing; this process will give an exceptional look to your portraits, they will have 'that something more that you can not explain'. Our fields of action in this area are glamor, nude, wedding, urban photography.
